Program description

This 6-hour workshop examines the Value Added Tax (VAT) framework in the UAE, with emphasis in the procedures, provisions and controls that management must have in place to ensure compliance with the VAT laws. Using an interactive methodology, participants will acquire the knowledge and tools to effectively manage the implementation of VAT in their organizations.

Program objectives

Upon completion of this course you will be able to:

  • Explain the characteristics of the UAE VAT framework, including its application and impact within the UAE economy.
  • Define the VAT concept, its treatment of supplies and exemptions.
  • Explain the rules for VAT Registration and De-registration.
  • Perform input and output VAT calculations and identify the possible sources of error.
  • Recognize the key administration and record-keeping tasks associated with the VAT process.
  • Calculate the VAT on foreign trade within and outside the GCC.
  • Recognise the implications and risks associated with VAT within a business' organizational functions.

Program details

VAT FRAMEWORK IN THE UAE
  • GCC and UAE VAT Framework.
  • Impact of VAT on UAE economic activity.
  • Distinctive characteristics of UAE VAT.

SCOPE OF THE UAE VAT FRAMEWORK
  • The VAT concept, objectives and process.
  • Treatment of input and output VAT .
  • Goods, services, gifts and samples .
  • Reverse and Forward Charge Mechanisms.
  • Taxable and non-taxable supplies.
  • Determining the place of supply.
  • Zero-rated supplies vs Exempt supplies.
  • Tax-exempted imports.

VAT REGISTRATION
  • Key definitions.
  • Rules for VAT registration and de-registration.
  • VAT groups and inter-group transactions.

CALCULATION OF VAT
  • Input and output VAT and net VAT payable.
  • VAT calculation errors.
  • Valuation of supplies.
  • VAT schemes in the UAE.
VAT ADMINISTRATION AND RECORD KEEPING
  • Filing VAT returns and claiming for VAT refunds.
  • Transitional provisions.
  • Preparing for VAT audits.
  • Administrative process for VAT assessments, fines and penalties for tax evasion.
  • General requirements of the VAT invoice.

VAT ON FOREIGN TRADE
  • Calculating output VAT on imports and exports.
  • VAT in intra-GCC trade.

IMPLICATIONS OF VAT FOR UAE BUSINESSES
  • Implication of VAT on organizational functions.
  • Minimizing risks associated with poor implementation.
