Program description

This 6-hr workshop examines the Value Added Tax (VAT) framework in the UAE, with emphasis in the procedures, provisions and controls that management must have in place to ensure compliance with the VAT laws. Using an interactive methodology, participants will acquire the knowledge and tools to effectively manage the implementation of VAT in their organizations.

Program objectives

Upon completion of this course you will be able to:

  • Explain the chacracteristics of the UAE VAT framework, including its application and impact within the UAE economy.
  • Define thethe VAT concept, its treatment of supplies and exemptions.
  • Explain the rules for VAT Registration and De-registration.
  • Perform input and output VAT calculations and identify the possible sources of error.
  • Recognize the key administration and record-keeping tasks associated with the VAT process.
  • Calculate the VAT on foreign trade within and outside the GCC.
  • Recognise the implications and risks associated with VAT within a business' organizational functions

Target audience

  • Accounting and Finance Managers
  • Procurement, Operations and Logistics managers
  • Marketing Managers
  • Legal and Compliance Managers
