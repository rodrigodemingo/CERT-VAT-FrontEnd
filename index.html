VAT in the UAE

Federal Decree-Law No.8 of 2017 on Value Added Tax (VAT) has been issued by the UAE Government, making 1st January 2018 the effective date of VAT implementation. All businesses with revenue exceeding the yearly mandatory threshold for VAT are required to register for VAT and be in compliance with the law. The Centre of Excellence for Applied Research and Training (CERT) is committed to supporting the needs of the nation and the public and private sectors through a portfolio of VAT training programs. Our customized programs in VAT implementation are designed for the specific needs of the different business functions, from Accounting and Finances to Operations, Logistics and Information Systems.

Executive Workshop on UAE VAT Implementation

This 6 hours workshop examines the Value Added Tax (VAT) framework in the UAE, with emphasis in the procedures, provisions and controls that management must have in place to ensure compliance with the VAT laws. Using an interactive methodology, participants will acquire the knowledge and tools to effectively manage the implementation of VAT in their organizations.
